Why Choose HealthVision AI?

A unified platform combining computer vision and deep learning for rapid, non-invasive health screening across three critical diagnostic areas.

👁️

Smart Eye Detection

Haar cascade algorithm with dual-strategy detection: face-first and direct eye scanning with 35% padding for optimal sclera extraction.

📷

Real-Time Camera

WebRTC camera integration with ROI guide overlay for precise image capture. Works on desktop and mobile browsers.

🧠

Deep Learning Models

MobileNetV2 and EfficientNetB0 transfer learning architectures pre-trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned for medical classification.

⚡

Instant Results

Confidence scores, top-k predictions, and detected region previews generated in under 2 seconds per analysis.

🤖

AI Health Assistant

Context-aware assistant generates scan summary, precautions, diet suggestions, urgent-sign guidance, and doctor-visit checklist from your latest result.

🏥

Hospital Finder + Booking UI

Search by state and district, view hospital cards, open maps, and create printable appointment receipts in the assistant workflow.

🛡️

Upload Stability Hardening

Input-size limits, large-image downscaling, and TensorFlow-safe server startup reduce Render 502 failures for file uploads.

🔒

Privacy First

All processing happens locally on the server. No images are stored, shared, or transmitted to third-party services.

📱

Fully Responsive

Works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile phones with an adaptive UI that fits any screen size.

⚙️ Pipeline

How It Works

A four-step pipeline from image capture to clinical prediction.

📸

Capture or Upload

Use your device camera with ROI guide or upload a photo of the eye, face, or nail.

👁️

Region Detection

OpenCV Haar cascades locate the region of interest (eye sclera, face, nail) with intelligent fallback strategies.

🧠

AI Analysis

The detected region is preprocessed (224×224, normalized) and fed to the appropriate deep learning model for inference.

📊

Get Results

Receive instant diagnosis: prediction label, confidence percentage, top-k classes, and detected region preview.

🤖

Ask Assistant

Open AI assistant for contextual Q&A, hospital finder, and booking support based on the same scan result timeline.
